Classic Waffles

Description

Crispy on the outside and soft on the inside,
these classic waffles are a timeless breakfast
favorite. Made with simple ingredients, they’re
perfect served warm with butter, syrup, or your
favorite toppings for a comforting and delicious
start to the day.

Ingredients

  • 2-cups all purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt or to taste
  • 4 tablespoons baking powder
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 1⁄2 cups warm milk
  • 1⁄3 cup butter, melted
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Mix flour, salt, baking power and sugar together in a large
    bowl; set aside. Preheat waffle iron to desired temperature.
  3. Beat eggs in a separate bowl; stir in milk, butter and vanilla.
  4. Pour milk mixture into flour mixture; beat until blended.
  5. Laddle batter into preheated waffle iron.
  6. Cook waffle until golden and crips.
  7. Serve immediately and enjoy!
